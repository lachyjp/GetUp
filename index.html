About

This is a project that utilises the Australian neobank Up API. After inputting your personal API key, this page allows you to view the balances of all your accounts, and details of your last 20 transactions.


Instructions

To view your own data, access your Up API key by following the link at the Up official documentation here and follow the instructions. You can then input your personal token after clicking "Log in" below.


Data privacy

By providing your key, you understand that all personal data is only transmitted between Up's servers and your local device, and that your key is not stored or transmitted to other parties. However, the project is not responsible if you accidentally expose or share your key. Follow Up's TOS and guidelines about data safety and sharing your key.
